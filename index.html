Leikhöllin

A community-built indoor play hall for children in Vestmannaeyjar, Iceland.

Leikhöllin is a grassroots initiative creating a permanent indoor play space for children and families in a remote island community.

Why it matters

Vestmannaeyjar is a small island community with long winters and limited indoor facilities for children. An indoor play hall is essential infrastructure for wellbeing, social connection, and family life.

What already exists

  • Core indoor play equipment secured
  • Active community involvement
  • Local fundraising in progress
