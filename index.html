Why it matters

Vestmannaeyjar is a small island community with long winters and limited indoor spaces for children. An indoor play hall is not a luxury here — it is essential infrastructure for wellbeing, play, and social connection.

What already exists

  • Core indoor play equipment secured
  • Strong community involvement
  • Active local fundraising
